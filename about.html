About Whiteboard

Whiteboard is a collaborative app, allowing you to share ideas. With the app, users can create and join boards, then upload submissions which can be viewed by all participants in the board.

Features of Whiteboard

Whiteboard offers many features not available on other whiteboarding sites -- the best part is that it is 100% free! Our site has:

↳ Unlimited boards, 100% free!

↳ Unlimited users and submissions.

↳ Real-time site updates between users.

↳ Save boards for next time!

↳ Edit and delete submissions.

↳ And So Much More!
